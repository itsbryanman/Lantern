# Family Guide

Lantern gives the household one simple dashboard at `https://your-domain`.

## Start here

1. Open the dashboard bookmark on your phone, tablet, or laptop.
2. Sign in with the account the administrator created for you.
3. Tap the app you want. Photos, media, recipes, and files each open in their own space.

## The main apps

- Photos: phone backup, shared albums, and browsing old memories.
- Movies & TV: stream the family library on TVs, phones, and tablets.
- Recipes: save meals, plan dinners, and keep shopping lists together.
- Files: open shared folders without needing to understand the server.

## Phone photo backup

1. Install the Immich mobile app.
2. Sign in with your household account.
3. Turn on automatic backup.
4. Leave the app open the first time so it can upload everything.

## If something looks wrong

- Dashboard does not load: tell the administrator the main site is down.
- Sign-in fails: ask the administrator to reset your account or password.
- Photos or videos are missing: try refreshing the app, then report the exact item and date range that is missing.
